Refund Policy

Effective Date: April 2026

1. 30-Day Money-Back Guarantee

We stand behind Postevo Premium. If you are not satisfied with your purchase, you may request a full refund within 30 days of your first subscription payment.

  • Full refund within 30 days of first purchase
  • No questions asked
  • Applies to the first subscription period only

2. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please contact us by email:

  • Email: postify.support@gmail.com
  • Include: Your order ID (found in your Polar.sh confirmation email) and an optional reason
  • Response time: We will respond within 2 business days

3. Processing Time

Once approved, refunds are processed by Polar.sh and typically take 5–10 business days to appear on your original payment method, depending on your bank or card issuer.

4. Eligibility

  • The 30-day guarantee applies to the first subscription period only
  • Subsequent monthly renewal charges are not eligible for refund
  • Partial refunds for mid-period cancellations are not available

5. Non-Refundable Cases

Refunds will not be issued in the following circumstances:

  • Your account has been suspended or terminated due to a violation of our Terms of Service
  • The refund request is made after the 30-day window has passed
  • The charge is a renewal (not the first payment)

6. How to Cancel

You may cancel your Postevo Premium subscription at any time:

  • Log in to the Polar.sh customer portal with your subscription email
  • Navigate to your active subscriptions and cancel
  • No cancellation fees apply
  • You retain access to Premium features until the end of your current billing period

7. Contact

For any questions about refunds or cancellations, contact us at: postify.support@gmail.com
